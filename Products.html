TimeBox - Clock that spells out time

Rugged and elegant design that combines thick layer of wood and a front glass panel makes it perfect for a desk clock. 

This clock works in a way that it spell out time for you in five minute intervals and lights those words out in any color your want. Additional markings on the clock make it possible to read time in one-minute intervals!

Fully customizable

Since it's entirely handmade, you can order it in a color that fits your environment and even ask for a specific set of light colors to be coded just for you!
There's a 15 character customisable space at the end of the glass panel that lets you order a dedicated message that will light up at the certain time of the day!

Main Features

Elegant Design

Precicely cut and glues pieces of wood with a magnetic interchangeable glass panel that sits on the front

Customized settings

Pick colors that fit your surroundings!

Remembers Time

Even if there is no electricity, it will not lose time next time you turn it on

Personalized message

Perfect clock to give as a gift to anyone!
